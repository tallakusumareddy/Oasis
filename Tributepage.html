Virat Kohli

Birth information

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 to Prem Kohli and Saroj Kohli in Delhi, India. His father was a criminal lawyer while his mother is a housewife. He was bred in Delhi's Uttam Nagar .

Education

Virat Kohli did his schooling till 8 th grade at the Vishal Bharati Public School. Then he shifted to Saviour Convent in Paschim Vihar, to concentrate more in cricket.

Board memberships and affiliations

Virat Kohli is arguably the best batsman in world cricket across all three formats of the game. In the last three years or so, Kohli has worked very hard and broken many batting records in the game. Many believe that Kohli is India’s backbone in all three forms of the game. India’s batting-unit plays around Kohli and he anchors the innings more often than not. The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) Vice-President Rajeev Shukla on January 15 said that Virat Kohli’s contribution to Indian cricket has been immense and he respect his decision. Shukla’s remarks came after Kohli stepped down as India's Test captain after leading the side for seven years.
